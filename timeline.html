About us

Growing gold producer in Mongolia

Mongold is a growing pure play underground gold producer in Mongolia, operating the high-grade Naran Mandal underground mine. The company is committed to developing high-grade assets efficiently while creating long-term sustainable value for all stakeholders. Mongold’s flagship asset, Naran Mandal Gold Operations in Mongolia, is a high-grade, narrow-vein orogenic gold deposit.

Mongold’s flagship asset, Naran Mandal Gold Operations in Mongolia, is a high-grade, narrow-vein orogenic gold deposit. More than 50 vein structures have been identified on the property, with only three veins currently in production. Less than 12% of the known resource is being exploited, highlighting substantial upside through further exploration, investment, and technological upgrades.

In addition, Mongold holds 12 exploration licenses across Mongolia with potential for gold, base metals, and battery minerals.

The company is supported by an experienced international management team operating from Singapore, Ulaanbaatar, and onsite locations. Health, safety, and environmental stewardship are non-negotiable priorities embedded across all operations.

Timeline

At Mongold, we are proud of our history. We have overcome many challenges to become the company we are today. Our resilience will serve us well as our company continues to develop and grow.

1950

The first scientific surveys of the Naran Mandal deposit were carried out by Soviet State Russian geologists. These studies identified the presence of a significant economic gold resource.

1967

East German geologists conducted a drilling expedition and inferred an economic resource on at least 8 of the 54 known veins based on shallow drilling results.

1990

The Mongolian government issued a mining license for the Naran Mandal deposit, building on work carried out by Russian, German and Japanese scientists.

2003

Bayangazar LLC formed a joint venture with a Chinese partner and acquired the Naran Mandal deposit. A processing plant was commissioned in 2004 and mining commenced the same year.

2011

A consortium of high net worth British and American investors formed the company that would later become Mongold and began exploring gold opportunities in Asian frontier markets.

2014

Mongold acquired control of the Naran Mandal gold mine project through a joint venture agreement with a local partner.

2015

Mongold began investing into the Naran Mandal gold project, developing underground mining operations and commencing plant construction.

2017

The plant was fully commissioned and operations began. Trial production commenced alongside a JORC-compliant drilling campaign. Mongold also acquired 12 additional exploration licenses in Mongolia.

2020

The processing plant capacity at the Naran Mandal Gold Operations was expanded while off-mine and near-mine exploration and M&A activities continued.

2023

An ore sorter was installed and commissioned at the Naran Mandal Gold Operations site, significantly increasing plant efficiency.
