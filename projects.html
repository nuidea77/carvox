About us

Building a High-Grade Gold Producer

Mongold is a Central-Asian junior gold producer with its flagship gold operation, Naran Mandal, located in Mongolia. Naran Mandal Gold Operations is a high-grade, narrow vein, orogenic deposit. The Operations property hosts more than 50 vein structures, of which only 3 have been explored and brought into production. Less than 12% of the known resource is currently being exploited, leaving significant upside to be achieved through additional exploration, investment and upgrading of technology. Mongold also holds 12 exploration licenses across Mongolia which have the potential for gold, base metals and battery minerals.
